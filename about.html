Objectives

With the assistance of Foundations, Fortune 500 companies, Local Businesses,Community Leaders and the community at large, we plan on opening computer labs in every underprivileged and underserved community in New York City.

The computer labs will be located in community centers in underserved and underprivileged communities in New York City, primarily adjacent or in the proximity of NYCHA buildings.

Reason

The Digital Divide has been discussed for decades; however, Covid shed an even bigger light on the need to ingratiate and sustain the use of technology in underprivileged and underserved communities.

Plan of action

  • Fundraising
  • Purchase of devices (laptops/desktops
  • Have City Council members and/or their office assist with identifying and maintaining the computer labs in their districts
  • Securing wi-fi in the designated facilities for the computer labs
  • Implementation of infrastructure
  • Scheduling of programming (i.e. S.T.E.A.M. Courses, Microsoft Office Tutorials and Certification, Open Use for Patrons, etc.)
